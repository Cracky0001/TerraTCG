TerraTCG is a Terraria-themed Trading Card Game! To start your
TerraTCG journey, craft a deck box with 5 wood at a Workbench,
then talk to the Guide for a tutorial!
Currently, TerraTCG can be played with the following NPCs:
- the Guide
- the Merchant
- the Nurse
- the Arms Dealer
- the Goblin Tinkerer
- the Party Girl
- the Traveling Merchant
- the Clothier
- the Nerdy Slime
